What is to retrain?

Retraining is a functionality that allows you to upload cattle videos on the cloud to aid in keeping your livestock recognition system updated.

How does It Retrain?

First, create four new videos by taking 20 second clips of each cow on your farm preferably on different lighting conditions . Once the videos are created you will be able to add/upload them.

What are some retraining best practices?

  • Have a resolution of 780x780
  • Use common video format such as .MP4 only
  • Remain under the 5MB limit.
  • upright video angles look best
